About Norway

Norway is a Kingdom in North Europe along the arctic circle and borders Sweden, Finland, and Russia. There has been a unified kingdom in Norway since 872 AD. Its capital and largest city is Oslo.

People in Norway speak a variety of languages but mostly Norwegian and Sami are spoken.

Due to the Gulf Stream, Norway has on average higher temperatures and more rainfall than other northern nations.
